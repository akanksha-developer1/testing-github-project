Ask Me Directly →
How long does it take to build a website?
Most projects are completed within 7–14 business days depending on scope. Basic WordPress sites take about 7 days, while custom development projects with more features take 14–21 days. I always provide a clear timeline upfront before starting.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes! Every package includes revision rounds. Basic includes 2 revisions, Standard includes 3, and Premium includes unlimited revisions. My goal is your 100% satisfaction before final delivery.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. Every website I build is fully responsive and tested across all major devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile optimization is never an add-on; it's built into every project by default.
What do you need from me to get started?
To kick off your project, I'll need your branding assets (logo, colors), content (text and images, or I can help source/write these), and a clear brief about your goals. We'll go over everything in our initial consultation call.
Do you provide hosting and domain setup?
I can help recommend the best hosting options for your budget and requirements, assist with domain registration, and handle the complete server setup and deployment of your website as part of the project.
What is your payment structure?
I work on a 50% upfront, 50% on delivery model. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards. Full receipt and invoice provided for every transaction.
